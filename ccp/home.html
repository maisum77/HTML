welcome to ----Sugar&Crust

Sugar&Crust is a dessert shop offering a wide range of chocolate, vanilla, and caramel flavours. Our menu is always updated to include the latest and greatest flavors. Like a fantasy that feels unreal, every delicacy by ‘Sugar & Crust’ holds a charm that enchants with every bite, allowing one to be swept into a moment of bliss, making all your dessert creams come true! Founded in Lahore in 2020, the philosophy of Sugar & Crust Bakeshop was simple; to create connections through desserts while striking the ideal balance between flavor and feeling. Since the beginning, Sugar&crust has secured an irreplaceable spot in the hearts of people.
